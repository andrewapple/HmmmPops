What are HmmmmPops?

There is a room in my brain where I collect HmmmPops. HmmmPops are what I call information that tickles my brain or pokes my heart or hugs my soul. This site is dedicated to sharing HmmmPops I have collected over the years.

HmmmPops can come in many forms. Some of them are:

  • Quotes
  • Blurbs from books
  • Riffs from podcasts
  • Convos with friends
